Yoga Studio JAD

We are Jad yoga school that tries to teach in a traditional way so students
can progress at their own pace. We offer quality, compassionate teaching. We try to
ensure everyone feels welcome as we feel the idea of a supportive community is very
important. We usually work withhands-on-adjustments:if you feel uncomfortable
with this,just let us know, no questions or judgement.

Our shola is named 'Jamie Shola' inspired by the story of an ancient indian king
called Ashoka who built many 'dharma shola' to support wondering yogis.We hope
our Jamie shola will be a supportive place for you

Mysore Self-practice

At Jad Yoga london we teach astanga yoga in a traditional method known as
Mysore self practice, which originated in Mysore, south india. Student are taught
individually within a group environment and practice at their own pace with one-to
one advise and sometimes adjustment given by the teacher.

Working in this way allows you to focus on your breath and encourages a meditative
quality to your practise. Complete newbies are always welcome- join us for more
information on how to get started and what to expect.
